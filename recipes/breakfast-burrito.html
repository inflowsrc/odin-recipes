Breakfast Burrito with Potatoes and Chorizo

Description

This breakfast burrito with potatoes, chorizo, eggs, onions, and peppers tastes delicious! It's quick to make and a great breakfast to start your day. Serve with a side of salsa, sour cream, and fruit.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 pound fresh chorizo
  • 1/2 poblano pepper, chopped
  • 1/2 yellow onion, chopped
  • 1/2 red bell pepper, chopped
  • 2 Yukon gold potatoes, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1/3 cup half-and-half
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons butter or olive oil
  • 1/4 cup shredded cheese of any kind
  • 4 burrito-sized flour tortillas

Steps

  1. Heat a large skillet over medium heat. Add chorizo, poblano peppers, onions, and red bell pepper. Cook until vegetables are tender, 5-7 minutes. Remove skillet from heat.
  2. In a microwave-safe bowl, cook potatoes with a small amount of water until fork tender, about 7-10 minutes, stirring halfway through, and drain. Stir potatoes into the chorizo mixture.
  3. Whisk eggs, half-and-half, salt, and pepper together in a large bowl.
  4. Heat butter in a skillet over medium heat. Add egg mixture and scramble until eggs are cooked, about 3 minutes. Remove eggs from heat and fold in the cheese.
  5. Heat tortillas in the microwave for 20-30 seconds.
  6. Spoon the chorizo mixture in a line across the middle of a tortilla, then add a layer of scrambled eggs. Fold opposing edges of the tortilla to overlap the filling. Roll 1 of the opposing edges around the filling, creating a burrito. Enjoy!
